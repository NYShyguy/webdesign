Polar bear fact #1

Polar bears are currently labeled as a "vulnerable" species, meaning they're on track to become endangered very soon, and perhaps even extinct.

Polar bear fact #2

Without arctic sea ice, polar bears don't have access to their primary food source, which is passively hunting seals. They will starve if global warming continues.

Polar bear fact #3

Oil and gas companies drilling into the Arctic Refuge would assuredly lead to a loss in polar bear habitat and hunting area.
